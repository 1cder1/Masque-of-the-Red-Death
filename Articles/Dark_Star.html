Is this where we go when we die?

All I could see was a patch of black sky

And then I saw a darkened star

Above the hill, so very far

Off so it didst appear

Equidistant with that star of fear.

The only light that star gave was black

And it lit me no way back.

It seemed to all my sight

A dak star shining bright as night,

Beckoning to my conscious mind

To step into oblivion, see what I might find.
